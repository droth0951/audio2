‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What podcasts does Audio2 support?
A: Audio2 works with any podcast that has an RSS feed, including all content from Apple Podcasts. You can search by name or paste RSS feed URLs directly.
Q: Where are my video clips saved?
A: All generated videos are automatically saved to your iOS Photos app, making them easy to share on LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok, or any social platform.
Q: What video formats are available?
A: Audio2 creates videos in vertical (9:16) format, perfect for Instagram Stories, TikTok, and LinkedIn mobile. Square (1:1) format coming soon!
Q: How long can my clips be?
A: Clips can be up to 4 minutes long, which is optimal for social media engagement and platform requirements.
Q: Why can't I find a specific podcast?
A: Try searching with different keywords (podcast name, host name, or topic). You can also paste the RSS feed URL directly if you have it.
Q: Does Audio2 work offline?
A: Audio2 requires an internet connection to search and stream podcasts. However, once you start playing an episode, it will continue playing if you temporarily lose connection.

Audio won't play
Check your internet connection and ensure the podcast episode is available. Some episodes may have geographic restrictions.
App crashes during video generation
Make sure you have enough storage space on your device. Close other apps if needed and try again with a shorter clip.
Can't find generated video
Check your Photos app - videos are saved automatically. Look for the most recent video or search for "Audio2" in Photos.
