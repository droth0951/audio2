## Step-by-Step Instructions

### 1. Background (Dark Gradient)

1. Click on the canvas background
2. In the left panel, click "Background color"
3. Click "Gradient"
4. Select "Linear gradient" (vertical)
5. Set colors:
   - **Top**: `#0a0a0a` (very dark gray)
   - **Middle**: `#1a1a1a` (dark gray)
   - **Bottom**: `#252525` (lighter dark gray)

### 2. Podcast Name (Top Center)

1. Press **T** (text tool) or click "Text" in left sidebar
2. Click "Add a heading"
3. Type: `THE DAILY` (or your podcast name)
4. **Position**:
   - X: Center horizontally (use alignment guides)
   - Y: 60px from top
5. **Styling**:
   - Font: **Roboto** or **Montserrat**
   - Weight: **Medium** (500)
   - Size: **32px**
   - Color: **White** `#ffffff`
   - Letter spacing: **1.5**
   - Transform: **UPPERCASE**
   - Alignment: **Center**

### 3. Podcast Artwork (Left Side, Centered)

1. Click "Elements" â†’ "Shapes" â†’ "Square"
2. Drag to canvas
3. **Size**: 400 Ã— 400 px (check top toolbar for exact sizing)
4. **Position**:
   - X: 96px from left edge
   - Y: 340px from top (vertically centered in frame)
5. **Styling**:
   - Corner rounding: **20px** (use slider in "Corner rounding")
   - Fill: `#1a1a1a` (placeholder - replace with actual artwork)
   - Border: 2px solid white at 10% opacity
6. **Add shadow**:
   - Click "Effects" â†’ "Shadows" â†’ "Drop shadow"
   - Offset X: 0, Y: 4
   - Blur: 8
   - Color: Black at 50% opacity

**To add actual artwork:**
- Drag artwork image into Canva
- Right-click artwork shape â†’ "Set image as background"
- Or: Upload image â†’ drag onto shape

### 4. Episode Title (Right Side, Top)

1. Add text box (Press **T**)
2. Type your episode title (e.g., "How AI Is Transforming the Way We Create Content")
3. **Position**:
   - X: 624px from left (use positioning tool)
   - Y: 200px from top
4. **Size**: Width: 1200px (right-click â†’ "Resize" or drag)
5. **Styling**:
   - Font: **Roboto** or **Open Sans**
   - Weight: **Regular** (400)
   - Size: **28px**
   - Line height: **1.2** (33.6px)
   - Color: **White** `#ffffff`
   - Alignment: **Left**
   - Max lines: Let it wrap naturally (2-3 lines)

### 5. Caption Line 1 (Right Side, Middle)

1. Add text box (Press **T**)
2. Type: `TODAY WE'RE HEARING FROM`
3. **Position**:
   - X: 624px from left
   - Y: 480px from top
4. **Styling**:
   - Font: **Arial** or **Helvetica** (bold)
   - Weight: **Extra Bold** (800)
   - Size: **64px**
   - Color: **White** `#ffffff`
   - Alignment: **Left**
5. **Add text shadow**:
   - Click "Effects" â†’ "Shadows" â†’ "Drop shadow"
   - Offset Y: 2px
   - Blur: 3px
   - Color: Black at 80% opacity

### 6. Caption Line 2 (Highlighted Word)
